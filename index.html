About the Event

TechSpark 2025 is a one-day event designed to bring together students, innovators, and tech enthusiasts from across the region. The event provides a platform to explore new ideas, showcase talents, and interact with industry experts through workshops, talks, competitions, and exhibitions. From coding challenges to creative projects, TechSpark encourages participants to collaborate, learn, and grow in a vibrant and inclusive environment. Whether you're here to compete, exhibit, or simply be inspired, this event is your launchpad into the world of innovation.
